Sales | Sales Item

Service to manage entities of type 'SalesItem'.

This service extends Items.

Overview


api/{tenantKey}/{orgKey}/salesCore/salesItems
URL api/{tenantKey}/{orgKey}/salesCore/salesItems
Methods Supported GET, POST, PUT, DELETE
