Master Data Business Entities | Assortment

Overview

api/{tenantKey}/{orgKey}/businessCore/assortments
URL api/{tenantKey}/{orgKey}/businessCore/assortments
Methods Supported GET, POST, PUT, DELETE
Description Service to manage entities of type 'Assortment'. Classification of the item in big families. For example, in a hospitality industry company, we can have the items grouped in kitchen items, office items, decoration items, electrical devices etc.

remarks (string) The remarks for the item assortment.
