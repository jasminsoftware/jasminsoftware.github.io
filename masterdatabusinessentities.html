Master Data Business Entities

The Master Data services API exposes common business entities management, such as data from base entities (parties and items). A base entity such as a party may be extended by other business areas. For instance, a party can be extended as a customer in the sales area, or as a supplier in the purchases area.

Services

This area of the Web API includes the following services:

  • Assortment
  • Model
  • Brand
  • Exchange Rates Document
  • Item
  • Organization Validation
  • Entity
  • Serie
