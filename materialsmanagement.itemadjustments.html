Materials Management | Item Adjustment

Service to manage entities of type 'ItemAdjustment'.

api/{tenantKey}/{orgKey}/materialsManagement/itemAdjustments

    • DELETE ItemAdjustment by identifier
    • DELETE ItemAdjustment by key
